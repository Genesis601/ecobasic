About EcoBasic

With over 20 years of experience in large-scale agriculture, EcoBasic specializes in mechanized farming solutions that optimize production and minimize environmental impact.

Our fleet of heavy-duty equipment, including John Deere tractors, boom sprayers, harrows, and planters, allows us to cultivate thousands of acres with precision and efficiency.

We combine cutting-edge technology with sustainable farming practices to deliver exceptional results for our clients and partners.
